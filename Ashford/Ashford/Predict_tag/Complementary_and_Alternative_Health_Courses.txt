<description> Introduction to Chinese medicine is the study of the medical system and healing practices traditionally used in China , and more recently , in the United States and other countries . The course endeavors to explore the conceptual framework of Chinese medicine ; in particular , from a contextual and historical perspective . Additionally the course will focus on how Chinese Medicine understands the concepts of health and disease , creating context for how health imbalances are understood in the medical system . </description> <prerequisite> Prerequisites : HCS 321 and HCS 326 </prerequisite>
